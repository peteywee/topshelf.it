How we think

The principles behind our work.

Everything we build is grounded in a few operating beliefs we've tested across real service businesses.

Documentation is a leadership tool.

A written process is the highest-leverage thing a service business owner can create. It removes you from the critical path and makes every hire more valuable.

Standards beat supervision.

You can't watch everything. A well-designed standard runs on its own — in the field, without a manager present, at 6 am on a Monday.

Systems enable people.

Great people leave companies with bad systems. Good systems make average people excellent and let your best people focus on the work that actually requires judgment.
